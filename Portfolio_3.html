Skills in making translation and subtitles!

My little music video, with my English and Vietnamese translation as Subtitle!

Nowadays, subtitles plays an important part in movies, videos. You can find them in almost movies in the theater around the world and videos across Youtube. Subtitles make videos more friendly and help people know what the meaning of the movies even if they don't know many languages!

How did I make it?

Aegisub is a well-known software to make subtitles (and animations as well, I included some in the viddeo, enjoy). Furthermore, it has built-in translator assistant. However, you must have knowlege about languages to make a good translation! For me, I learned Japanese back in highschool and Vietnamese is my mother tongue.
For the best quality, I used VirtualDub Studio to stick the subtitles to the video.
